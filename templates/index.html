Hufflepuff

Hufflepuffs in the Harry Potter universe are known for their strong sense of loyalty, hard work, dedication, fairness, and a belief in teamwork. They value these traits above all else and are often characterized as humble, kind, and patient. Hufflepuffs are also known for their dedication to what they do and their focus on doing things properly.
